simple-freesound

Simple library for Node.js and the browser offering an intuitive interface to access the basic functionalities of freesound api.

Its main goal is to ease the process of querying sounds from a few search terms and filters and downloading their hq mp3 previews.

It is composed of two classes, client/SimpleFreesound and server/SimpleFreesound. Both are very similar, except the download methods which keep the resulting audio buffers as local variables in the client class, and download the hq mp3 preview files on the hard drive in the server class.
